Knapsack Game
Goal of the Game
 Your goal is to maximize the value in your knapsack. This is done by choosing the most valuable combination of boxes to fill the knapsack. The optimal combined value of the knapsack is 100% for every round. With an empty knapsack you start out at 0% of the knapsack value. By clicking the available boxes in the upper area the value of the knapsack increases and you get closer and closer to the optimal combined value of 100%.
Max. result: 100%
Payout Scheme
 For each completed round, you get a guaranteed $0.05 payout. If you achieve a result greater then or equal to 80%, you get an extra bonus of up to $0.10 the closer you approach 100%. If you do not select any boxes during one round, you will not receive any payout.

The Dilemma

By adding and removing boxes in the knapsack you can evaluate different combinations. Boxes with high value and large size can be traded for boxes with lower value and smaller size or vice versa.
